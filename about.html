We Fix What's Stopping Customers
From Reaching Out.

CoreX was built to help service businesses get more calls, messages, and inquiries. Most service businesses are losing leads without realizing it — people who search for them on Google, land on their site, or reach out and never hear back. We look at your business the same way a customer would, from the moment they search to the moment they decide whether to contact you, and we fix what's in the way.

The Story

Why We Started CoreX

We kept seeing the same problem.

Good service businesses — the HVAC companies, the roofers, the flooring contractors, the tradespeople who actually show up and do great work — were losing customers they didn't even know they were losing. People who searched for them on Google and found someone else. People who landed on their site and left without calling. Leads that came in and never got followed up on.

The work was good. The business was struggling to grow. And most of the time, the owners had no idea why.

That's the problem CoreX was built to fix.

The Founder

Cris L.

Founder, CoreX Solutions · Peachtree Corners, GA

The team behind CoreX brings together over two decades of operations leadership and over a decade inside the tech industry — startups, enterprise integrations, and the kind of tools and systems that well-resourced companies use to find customers, earn their trust, and grow.

We know how operations actually work. We know what it takes to position a business so the right people find it and understand it. We know how customers think, what makes them hesitate, and what makes them pick up the phone. And we know how to use technology to make all of it work — quietly, in the background, without getting in the way of the actual work.

Most small businesses never get access to that combination. The experience, the strategy, the tools — it all lives inside big companies and well-funded startups. The roofer, the HVAC tech, the flooring contractor doing great work every day is left to figure it out alone.

That's the gap CoreX was built to close. We take everything we've built and learned and we put it to work for the businesses that deserve it most — simply, practically, and without the corporate price tag. You focus on the work you're great at. We make sure the right customers can find you, trust you, and reach out.
