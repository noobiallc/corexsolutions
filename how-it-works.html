01

The Intro Call

We start with a quick call — usually 15 to 20 minutes. No pitch, no presentation, no pressure. We ask about your business, where your leads are coming from today, and what's getting in the way of getting more. You talk, we listen.

✦ Takes 15–20 minutes ✦ No jargon, no sales pitch ✦ You tell us about your business and your biggest frustrations ✦ We figure out if we're the right fit for each other
02

The Growth Review

After the call, we do a thorough review of your website and your Google presence — the same way a potential customer would. We look at what's unclear, what's making people hesitate, what's making you hard to find, and what's making it harder than it should be to reach out. Then we put together clear, specific feedback on what's costing you the most calls and inquiries right now.

✦ We review your website and Google presence ✦ We look at it through the eyes of a potential customer ✦ We identify what's costing you the most leads ✦ You get clear, specific feedback — not a generic report ✦ Most reviews are ready within 1–2 business days
03

Once you've seen the review and you're ready to move forward, we get to work. We fix what's actually costing you leads — whether that's unclear messaging, a weak Google presence, a site that doesn't make it easy to reach out, or leads that are slipping through once they come in. You'll know exactly what we're fixing and what it costs before we start. No surprises.

What We Fix

What We Actually Fix

Every business is different. But here are the most common things getting in the way of more calls and inquiries.

✦ Unclear Messaging

People land on your site and can't quickly figure out what you do, who you serve, or why they should call you. We fix that so the right people know immediately that you're the right choice.

✦ Weak Google Presence

You're not showing up when people search for businesses like yours — or your listing has wrong information, no photos, or too few reviews. We fix what's making you invisible or untrustworthy on Google.

✦ Hard to Contact

People are interested but reaching out feels like work — the phone number is buried, the form is complicated, or there's no obvious next step. We make it simple for someone to call or message you in seconds.

✦ Poor First Impression

Your business doesn't look trustworthy or credible at first glance — so people leave before they even read a word. We fix how your business comes across so people feel confident before they pick up the phone.

✦ Missed Leads

Inquiries are coming in but some are getting missed — calls going unanswered, messages sitting for too long, leads going cold. We help make sure every inquiry gets handled properly.

✦ No Consistent Lead Flow

Some weeks are busy, some are dead. There's no reliable, steady stream of calls and inquiries coming in. We fix the root causes so your lead flow becomes something you can count on.
