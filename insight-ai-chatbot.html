Most small business websites have the same quiet problem: visitors arrive, browse for a minute, and leave without ever reaching out. Not because they weren't interested. Because they had a question and no one was there to answer it.

That gap — between the moment someone is curious and the moment they decide to move on — is where most small business leads disappear. It's not a traffic problem. It's a response problem.

CoreX has built an AI chatbot solution designed specifically for small business websites. It sits on your site, ready to answer questions around the clock, qualify leads, and guide interested visitors toward booking or reaching out — without requiring you to be available at every hour of the day.

"Small businesses lose leads every day because they can't respond fast enough. AI chat keeps them in the conversation — even when the team isn't available."

Why Response Speed Matters More Than You Think

When a potential customer visits your website and has a question — about your services, your availability, your process — they're not going to wait until the next business day to find out. They're going to do one of two things: get an answer right now, or move on and find someone else who can help them.

Most small businesses can't respond to inquiries instantly. That's not a criticism — it's just reality. You're running a business, not monitoring a chat window. But every hour that passes between a visitor's question and your response is an hour they're spending on a competitor's site.

The numbers are stark. Responding to a lead within the first hour makes you dramatically more likely to close the job than responding the next day. After a few hours, the odds drop significantly. By the next morning, many of those leads have already made a decision — just not with you.

The after-hours problem: A large portion of website traffic happens outside of normal business hours — evenings, weekends, early mornings. Without a way to engage those visitors, you're invisible to a significant chunk of the people who are actively looking for you.

What the CoreX AI Chatbot Does

CoreX's chatbot is a text-based AI that handles the first part of every conversation — answering common questions, collecting contact details, and guiding visitors toward the next step. Here's what it does specifically:

Answers questions immediately

When a visitor wants to know about your services, your hours, your process, or what to expect, the chatbot responds in plain, natural language. It feels like chatting with a knowledgeable team member — not clicking through a FAQ page or waiting for a callback.

Captures lead information

Instead of relying on a contact form that visitors may or may not fill out, the chatbot collects names, contact details, and qualifying information through natural conversation. Those details get passed to you with full context so you can follow up effectively.

Supports appointment booking

For businesses that take appointments or consultations, the chatbot guides visitors toward scheduling or requesting a callback — turning a browsing visitor into a confirmed lead before they ever leave the page.

Stays active 24/7

When you're on a job, handling an existing customer, or simply done for the day — the chatbot stays active. No more missed after-hours inquiries. No more visitors leaving because no one was available to answer a simple question.

Customized to your business

CoreX tailors each chatbot to reflect the specific services you offer, the tone of your brand, and the steps you want visitors to take. It's configured for your business — not built from a generic template.

"Instead of letting visitors leave with unanswered questions, businesses can give them immediate help and a clear next step."

The Real Problem This Solves

Most small business websites are passive. They display information. They wait for visitors to decide to reach out. They hope the contact form gets used.

That passivity costs money. When someone is on your site and interested, that moment has value. The longer it takes to acknowledge their interest and answer their questions, the more likely they are to move on. An AI chatbot makes your website active instead of passive — it reaches out to visitors who are browsing, answers the questions that were stopping someone from calling, and collects the information you need to follow up effectively.

For a small business already getting website traffic, this is often the single highest-leverage improvement available. You're not spending more money to get more visitors. You're converting more of the visitors you already have.

Who This Is For

The CoreX chatbot is built for small service businesses — HVAC contractors, roofers, plumbers, landscapers, cleaning services, consultants, and any business where new customer inquiries come through a website.

If you're getting website visitors but not capturing as many leads as you should, or if you know you're missing after-hours inquiries, or if you just want a faster and more responsive way to handle incoming interest — this is designed for exactly that situation.
